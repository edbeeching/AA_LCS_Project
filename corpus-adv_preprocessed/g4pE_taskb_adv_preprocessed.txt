pagerankalgorithm is also known as link analysis algorithm. it has been used by google. the algorithm may be applied to any collection of entities with reciprocal quotations and hyperlinked set of documents, such as the world wide web, with the purpose of measuring references. the name pagerank is a trademark of google, and the pagerank process has been patented u.s. patent 6,285,999 . the numerical weight that it assigns to any given element e is also called the pagerank of e and denoted by pre.

in other words, a pagerank results from a ballot among all the other pages on the world wide web about how important a page is. a hyperlink to a page counts as a vote of support. the pagerank of a page is defined recursively and depends on the number and pagerank metric of all pages that link to it incoming links.
